Gauteng, South Africa

Gauteng is a fast paced, dynamic province characterized by a dense urban development, economic opportunity and cultural diversity. It is often described as the engine of South Africa's economy and a focal point of innovation, migration and social change. Gauteng's development was shaped by the discovery of gold in the late 19th century, which triggered rapid urbanization and industrial growth.
