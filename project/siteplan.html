Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide individuals with clear, practical, and personalized guidance on healthy eating. It will serve as a hub where visitors can access balanced meal plans, nutrition tips, and resources tailored to different goals such as weight management, fitness performance, or overall wellness. The site aims to simplify healthy living by offering easy-to-follow recipes, educational articles, and interactive tools that empower users to make sustainable dietary choices.

Scenarios

  • John, a 30-year-old office worker, wants to lose 10 pounds before summer. He visits the site to find a beginner-friendly meal plan and track his calorie intake.
  • What easy but healthy week day meal can I make?
